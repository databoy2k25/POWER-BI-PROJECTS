# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project is a Power BI dashboard that provides insights into HR metrics such as headcount, average salary, leave balance, and demographic details across various job titles.

## Features
- **Average Leave Balance (LBL):** Displays the overall average leave balance for employees.
- **Average Salary:** Highlights the average salary across the organization.
- **Headcount:** Shows the total number of employees.
- **Headcount Analysis:**
  - By Age and Gender
  - By Job Title
  - By Gender
- **Leave Balance Insights:** Compares leave balances by job titles.
- **Salary and Qualification Relationship:** Visualizes the correlation between salary and educational qualifications.

## Visualizations
- **Bar Charts:** Used for headcount and leave balance comparisons.
- **Pie Chart:** Displays gender distribution.
- **Scatter Plot:** Represents the salary and qualification relationship.
- **Key Metrics:** Highlights average salary, total headcount, and leave balance over 20 days.
